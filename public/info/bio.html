Bio

b. 1993 Gainesville, Florida

Sarah Meftah is an artist currently living in Tallahassee, FL. Her photographic works explore image-making, as well as, survey the relationship between the a man and the landscape, and fashion as cultural phenomenon. Her individual projects have been exhibited at FSU's Museum of Fine Arts, Phyllis Straus Gallery, and published in Six Catalogue.
