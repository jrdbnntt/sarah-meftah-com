For a Very Brief Moment

This body of work is a series of photographs that explore commonplace moments at a heightened gaze. They are social documentary-based works that address questions of perception, observation, and truth in photography. Given their cinematic characteristics, images could be read as open ended visual shorts. As a collection, For a Very Brief Moment is pure documentation of reality and it's everyday strangeness.

For a Very Brief Moment will be available to purchase as a booklet via St. Sebastian Press in May 2016.
